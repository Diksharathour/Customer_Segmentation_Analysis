## **Customer Segmentation & Consumer Behavior Analysis**


## **Project Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing customer demographics, purchasing behavior, spending patterns, and customer value segmentation using SQL and Power BI.The `Customer_sengemts` dataset includes detailed information such as Customer_ID, Year_Birth, Demographics,	Education, Marital_Status, Income, Dt_Customer, Recency and WebVisitsPerMonth.
The objective is to transform raw customer data into actionable business insights that can help organizations improve customer targeting, increase revenue, optimize marketing campaigns, and identify high-value customers.

## **Objectives**

1. **Database Setup-** Import and organize the Customer Segmentation dataset into a structured SQL database to enable efficient data storage, querying, and analysis of customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and revenue patterns.
2. **Data Cleaning and Preparation-** Perform comprehensive data quality checks by identifying and handling missing values, duplicate records, and inconsistencies to ensure accurate and reliable analytical results.
3. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)-** Analyze customer demographics, income levels, purchasing behavior and engagement metrics to uncover trends, patterns, and key drivers of customer spending.
4. **Customer Behavior Analysis Using SQL-** Utilize SQL queries to answer critical business questions, including:
- Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
- How do income, education, and marital status influence spending behavior?
- How does customer recency impact spending and engagement?
5. **Customer Segmentation and Value Assessment-** Develop a customer segmentation model by categorizing customers into High Value, Medium Value, and Low Value segments based on spending patterns, income levels, and purchasing behavior to support targeted marketing and customer retention strategies.
6. **Interactive Dashboard Development-** Build an interactive Power BI dashboard to monitor key customer analytics metrics, including revenue performance, customer segments, product category contribution, purchasing behavior, and retention indicators through dynamic visualizations and filters.
7. **Insights and Business Recommendations-** Generate actionable insights to help stakeholders understand customer behavior, identify high-value customer groups, optimize marketing efforts, improve customer retention, increase customer lifetime value, and support data-driven decision-making.

## **Business Problem**

Q.1 Which customer groups generate the highest revenue?

Q.2 What factors influence customer spending?

Q.3 Which products contribute the most to overall sales?

Q4. Which customers are at risk of disengagement?

Q.5. Which customer segments should marketing teams prioritize?

## **Key Business Insights**

- Wine products contribute the largest share of revenue.
- Graduates and higher-income customers generate the highest spending.
- Store purchases remain the most preferred purchasing channel.
- High Value customers contribute a significant portion of total revenue despite representing a smaller customer base.
- Medium Value customers represent the largest growth opportunity.

  ## **Business Recommendations**

1. **Customer Retention**
- Launch targeted campaigns for At-Risk customers.
- Implement loyalty programs for high-value customers.
  
2. **Revenue Growth**
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing customer segments.
- Promote top-performing product categories to maximize revenue.
  
3. **Channel Optimization**
- Improve online customer experience to increase web purchases.
- Use customer behavior insights to personalize marketing campaigns.
- Invest in the most profitable channel.

4. **Re-Engage At-Risk Customers**
- Send reminders based on previous purchase behavior.
- Implement automated email and SMS reactivation campaigns.

5. **Focus Marketing on High-Income Segments**
- Design premium product promotions targeting high-income groups.
- The business should identify and prioritize high-income customers.
